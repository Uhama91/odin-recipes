Ingredients for Lasagna (Serves 6-8)

For the Meat Sauce (Bolognese)

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) dry red wine (optional, for depth of flavor)
  • 2 cans (14 oz/400g each) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (to balance acidity)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (chopped, for added freshness)

For the Béchamel Sauce

  • 4 tablespoons (60g) unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup (30g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups (720ml) whole milk, warmed
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional, for a classic touch)
  • Salt and white pepper, to taste

For the Lasagna Assembly

  • 12-15 lasagna noodles (pre-cooked or no-boil)
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup (250g) ricotta cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)

Steps to Cook Lasagna

Step 1: Prepare the Meat Sauce (Bolognese)

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and sauté until softened.
  3. Stir in minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add ground beef, cook until browned, then drain excess fat.
  5. Pour in red wine (if using) and let it reduce slightly.
  6. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  7. Simmer for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add fresh basil at the end.

Step 2: Prepare the Béchamel Sauce

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Whisk in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes to form a roux.
  3. Gradually add warmed milk while whisking to prevent lumps.
  4. Cook until thickened, then season with nutmeg, salt, and white pepper.

Step 3: Cook the Lasagna Noodles

  1. Boil noodles in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. Drain and lay them flat to prevent sticking.

Step 4: Preheat the Oven

  1. Set your oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 5: Assemble the Lasagna

  1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  2. Add a layer of lasagna noodles.
  3. Spread 1/3 of the meat sauce and béchamel sauce over the noodles.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  5. Repeat the layers two more times, finishing with béchamel and cheese on top.

Step 6: Bake the Lasagna

  1. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
  3. Broil for 2-3 minutes for a crispier top (optional).

Step 7: Rest and Serve

  1. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before cutting.
  2. Garnish with fresh basil and serve warm.
